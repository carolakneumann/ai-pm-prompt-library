## 🧠 The One Meta-Principle to Remember

> AI can tell you what users said. 
> It cannot tell you what users meant. 
> The gap between stated preference and revealed preference 
> requires human judgment.

Every prompt in this library is designed around that boundary — 
AI handles synthesis and structure, you own the interpretive leap.
